Rhododendron (plural: rhododendra)

This website lists rhododendron species and cultivars with photos and information.

This website is still very much a work in progress. Stay tuned as functionality and data are regularly added.

This website aims to be a successor to the late www.hirsutum.com. It owes a debt of gratitude to Herman and Marjo Van Ree and to the Hirsutum contributors for over a decade of data collection and work.
