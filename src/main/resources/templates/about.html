About

Rhododendron (plural: rhododendra)

This website lists rhododendron species and cultivars with photos and information.

This website is still very early in it's development - there's much more on the way! Stay tuned as functionality and data are regularly added.

Special thanks to Sean Rafferty for his generosity, time, and wealth of advice. Sean first described to me www.hirsutum.info, its utility to the community, and it's unfortunate demise. That conversation became the origin of this project.
